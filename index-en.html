About Your Event

Your Event (Hadathak) is a pioneer Saudi company in the industry of organizing and managing events by specialized cadres of the talent of our youth and their influential national competence, in providing all types of services from implementing, organizing and marketing of exhibitions, conferences and events inside and outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, starting from the idea to your innovative event that last long in the minds and turn your goals into a tangible reality. This is done by the presence of cadres and talented national competencies with experience, who possess passion and challenge, by virtue of their participation in previous management of many major national events such as the King Abdulaziz Camel Festival, Okaz Historical Festival and Janadriyah National Festival.
